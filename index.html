Watch Spirit Away

Spirited Away is a Japanese animated fantasy film written and directed by Hayao Miyazaki. The film follows a young girl named Chihiro who becomes trapped in a spirit world and must find a way to return home.

Charater Information

Haku/White Dragon

Haku is a mysterious, dragon-like boy who helps Chihiro find inner strength in the spirit world, but also has his own agenda and is later revealed to be a river spirit named Kohaku who saved Chihiro's life.

Chihiro

Chihiro is a young girl who finds herself in the spirit world and must find inner strength to survive and eventually return home.

Zeniba

Zeniba is Yubaba's twin sister who lives in a cottage in the swamp and helps Chihiro on her journey to return home.

Yubaba

Yubaba is the powerful witch who runs the bathhouse in the spirit world and holds the key to Chihiro's return home.

"You must keep moving forward, no matter how difficult it is, or how painful it becomes. You must keep moving forward, because only then will you find your way home."

- Haku/The White Dragon
